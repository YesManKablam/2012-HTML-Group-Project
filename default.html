Welcome to The No.1 Tom Cosm Site.

Want to be able to write music but have no idea how? Want to hear interesting, new music for free? The you have come to the right place. This site is dedicated to helping others to learn how to write and produce music by providing free tutorials, and to spread free music from producer Tom Cosm. To learn more about Tom, go to the About page.

A Message From Tom.

"Well this is my favourite fan website. It started as a place to share my music, which then extended to sharing my knowledge, which then extended to people joining up and having a nice place to swap ideas.... and now it's a buzzing little community of likeminded people, what fun! "

A Message From Us.

We've put this site together to help point people in the right direction.
Learning to produce can be difficult and has a steep learning curve. Thanks to Tom it has become easier due to the resources he has made easily available on the net.
